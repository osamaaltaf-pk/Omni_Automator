Privacy Policy

Omni Automator Chrome Extension · Last Updated: May 16, 2026

Overview

Omni Automator ("the Extension") is a Chrome browser extension developed by Osama Altaf that automates AI image and video generation across Meta AI, Google Gemini, and ChatGPT. This Privacy Policy explains what data the Extension interacts with, why, and how it is handled. All processing happens locally in your browser. No data is sent to any external server owned or operated by us.

Data We Interact With

Data Type Why It's Accessed Stored?
Personally Identifiable Information Reads the username/profile shown on AI platform pages only to confirm an active logged-in session Never
Web History (Tab URLs) Reads the URL of active tabs to detect which AI platform is open and trigger the correct automation workflow Never
User Activity Simulates clicks and text input on AI platform pages to automate prompt submission and generation on your behalf Never
Website Content Reads AI platform page content to detect generation status, extract image outputs, and confirm workflow completion Never

What We Never Collect

  • Passwords or authentication credentials
  • Financial or payment information
  • Health information
  • Personal communications (emails, messages, chats)
  • Location data
  • Any data from websites other than Meta AI, Gemini, and ChatGPT

Local Storage

The Extension saves the following data exclusively on your device using Chrome's local storage — never on any external server:

  • Your prompt lists and generation history
  • Flow Pipeline queue and settings
  • Generation mode preferences
  • Premium status and subscription state

Permissions Explained

Permission Why It's Needed
activeTab To detect and interact with the active Meta AI, Gemini, or ChatGPT tab and automate generation
tabs To switch between AI platform tabs automatically during bulk generation workflows
scripting To inject automation scripts into AI platform pages to input prompts and trigger generation
downloads To read AI-generated images from your Downloads folder for the Flow Pipeline queue
storage To save prompt lists, settings, and preferences locally on your device
unlimitedStorage To store large prompt lists and pipeline data without hitting Chrome's default storage quota
sidePanel To display the Omni Automator control panel as a persistent side panel during automation
browsingData To clear cached session data when switching between AI platforms for clean automation runs
host permissions To run automation scripts specifically on meta.ai, gemini.google.com, and chatgpt.com

Third-Party Platforms

The Extension automates actions on Meta AI (meta.ai), Google Gemini (gemini.google.com), and ChatGPT (chatgpt.com). Your use of these platforms is governed by their own privacy policies. Omni Automator is not affiliated with Meta, Google, or OpenAI.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, lease, or transfer any user data to third parties under any circumstances. The Extension has no backend server, no external database, and no analytics tracking.

Children's Privacy

This Extension is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the Extension evolves. Changes will be reflected with an updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
